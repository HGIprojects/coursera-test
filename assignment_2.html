What's on the menu today?

Okonomiyaki

Okonomiyaki is a popular Japanese savory pancake, packed with a delicious mix of cabbage, meat or seafood, and topped with tangy okonomiyaki sauce, mayonnaise, and bonito flakes. Often referred to as "Japanese pizza," it's a flavorful and satisfying dish that's fun to eat and make!

Fried Eggs

A comforting plate of fried eggs and fries offers a delightful combination of crispy, golden fries alongside perfectly fried eggs with runny yolks. Each bite delivers a harmonious blend of savory and salty flavors, making it an irresistible classic.

Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of flat pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all baked to bubbly perfection. Perfect comfort food with every bite offering a delicious blend of flavors and textures.
